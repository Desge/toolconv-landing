About toolconv

toolconv is a collection of free online conversion tools for everyday file, image, PDF, and measurement tasks. The project is built around a simple idea: useful tools should be fast, understandable, and respectful of user privacy.

What we provide

  • Unit converters for length, weight, temperature, area, volume, speed, data, time, currency, and related measurement tasks.
  • Image tools for compression, resizing, cropping, format conversion, watermarks, metadata removal, and other browser-based image workflows.
  • PDF tools for merging, splitting, compressing, organizing, and converting PDF files.

Our privacy-first approach

Wherever technically possible, toolconv tools run locally in your browser. That means your files are processed on your device instead of being uploaded to a server. Some pages may display advertising or use privacy-conscious analytics, but file processing is kept separate from advertising and analytics systems.

Publisher information

toolconv.com is the main publisher domain for the toolconv family of sites, including unit.toolconv.com, image.toolconv.com, and pdf.toolconv.com. Questions, corrections, partnership requests, and support messages can be sent through the contact page.
