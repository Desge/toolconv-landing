Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

This policy explains how toolconv.com and related toolconv subdomains handle information when you use our browser-based conversion tools.

File processing

Our tools are designed to process files locally in your browser whenever technically possible. Files you select for browser-based tools are not intentionally uploaded to toolconv servers for processing. Your files remain on your device unless a specific feature clearly states otherwise.

Information we may receive

  • Basic technical information sent automatically by your browser, such as IP address, user agent, referring page, and request time.
  • Messages and email addresses you choose to provide when contacting us.
  • Aggregated usage information from analytics or hosting providers, where enabled on a toolconv subdomain.

Advertising and cookies

toolconv may use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, device identifiers, and similar technologies to serve, measure, and personalize ads. You can learn more in Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy and manage ad settings at adssettings.google.com.

Analytics

Some toolconv subdomains may use privacy-conscious analytics or platform analytics to understand site performance and usage. Analytics data is used to improve reliability, content quality, and user experience. We do not use analytics to inspect your files.

Third-party links

toolconv pages may link to sibling toolconv subdomains, advertising partners, documentation, or other third-party websites. Those sites have their own privacy practices, and their policies apply when you visit them.

Your choices

  • You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings.
  • You can avoid selecting files if you do not want a tool to process them locally in your browser.
  • You can contact us to request deletion of support messages you previously sent.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@toolconv.com.
